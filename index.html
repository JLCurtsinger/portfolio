Bio

Hello! I’m Justin Curtsinger, a passionate front-end developer and creative professional with a background in Computer Information Technology. I specialize in UI/UX design, web development, and graphic design, blending technical expertise with a keen eye for detail. My goal is to help businesses and individuals bring their digital ideas to life through innovative and user-centered design solutions. With an Associate’s degree in Computer Information Technology and soon to complete my Bachelor’s in Graphic Information Technology at Arizona State University, I have honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, along with a solid foundation in graphic design using Adobe Creative Suite. I am also a U.S. Army combat veteran, which has instilled in me a strong work ethic, adaptability, and the ability to tackle challenges with a problem-solving mindset. After my service, I pursued a career in tech, working as a field service technician before diving deeper into the creative side of web development and design. I believe that great design not only looks good but also functions seamlessly to provide users with a memorable and intuitive experience. When I’m not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new places, running, or working on personal passion projects like creating games and interactive experiences. Let’s work together to make your digital dreams a reality!
